Smemorandum

The app to never forget birthdays, anniversaries and important deadlines

🍎 App Store ▶ Google Play

📱 Main features

  • Birthday, anniversary and recurring event reminders with sound notifications
  • Bill, appointment and renewal deadline tracking
  • Repeat alerts until end of day (1h–6h) — never forget a bill again
  • Integrated calendar with daily and monthly views
  • Multiple reminders per event (same day, day before, week before)
  • Custom notification sounds for each event
  • Automatic recurrences: yearly, monthly, weekly
  • Customizable categories with icons and colors
  • Birthday import from phone contacts
  • Data backup and restore
  • Light, dark and automatic theme
