# Privacy Policy — Smemorandum

**Last updated:** April 20, 2026

Smemorandum ("the app", "we") is a mobile application developed by
**Giampietro Leonoro** ("the developer"), available on iOS and Android.
This Privacy Policy describes what data the app processes, how, and what
your rights are.

**Summary: Smemorandum is an offline-first app. All your events
(birthdays, anniversaries, deadlines) are stored only on your device. We
do not create accounts, run our own servers, sell, or share personal
data.**

## 2. Data the app processes

### 2.1. Data stored on your device only

The following data is stored exclusively on your device (through the
app's local storage) and **is never transmitted to remote servers**:

- Created events (title, date, category, notes, reminder sound)
- Contacts imported from your address book (name, birthday, phone number
  — only if you use "Import contacts")
- App settings (theme, language, ad consent, sounds)
- Selected holidays
- Custom categories

When you uninstall the app, this data is removed from the device.

### 2.2. Permissions requested

The app requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | When requested | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Contacts** | Only when you use "Import contacts" | Read name and birthday to create birthday reminders. No data is transmitted. |
| **Notifications** | On first launch | Deliver local reminders scheduled on-device. |
| **Calendar** (optional, iOS) | Only if you enable calendar sync | Sync events with Apple Calendar. |

You can revoke each permission at any time from your device's
**system settings**.

### 2.3. Data we do NOT process

- **No user account**: we don't ask for email, password, or sign-up data.
- **No health, financial, or location data.**
- **No behavioral analytics** (no session tracking, screen views, taps).

## 3. Third-party services

### 3.1. Advertising (Google AdMob) — free users only

Free-tier users may see banner and interstitial ads from
**Google AdMob**. **Premium** users see no ads.

At first launch (or later in Settings) you can choose between:

- **Personalized ads** — AdMob may use anonymous advertising identifiers
  (IDFA / GAID) to show more relevant ads.
- **Non-personalized ads** (default in EU/UK) — AdMob receives only
  minimal technical data (country, language) to show generic ads.

Google AdMob privacy information:
[https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

Google AdMob is a service of Google Ireland Limited (EU) and Google LLC
(USA). Extra-EU transfers are covered by **Standard Contractual
Clauses** approved by the European Commission.

### 3.2. In-app purchases (RevenueCat + Apple / Google)

**Premium** subscriptions are handled through:

- **Apple In-App Purchase** (iOS) — [Apple privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- **Google Play Billing** (Android) — [Google privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **RevenueCat** — backend service that verifies purchase receipts.
  RevenueCat receives an anonymous device ID and receipt details (no
  personal data). Policy:
  [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

### 3.3. Crash reporting (optional, future)

The app includes scaffolding for anonymous crash reporting (Sentry). It
is **not active** at the moment and no crash data is sent. If activated
in the future, this policy will be updated and activation will be
mentioned in the release notes.

## 4. Legal basis (GDPR)

For EU users, the legal basis of processing is:

- **Contract performance** (art. 6.1.b GDPR) — for the app's core
  functionality (reminders, notifications, Premium purchases).
- **Consent** (art. 6.1.a GDPR) — for AdMob personalized ads. You can
  withdraw consent at any time from the app settings.
- **Legitimate interest** (art. 6.1.f GDPR) — for non-personalized ads
  (free users) and purchase verification.

---

## 5. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

- **Access** — export all your data in JSON format from the app
  Settings ("Export data").
- **Deletion** — delete all app data ("Reset data") or uninstall the
  app.
- **Portability** — the export file can be opened and imported into any
  compatible app.
- **Objection / consent withdrawal** — turn off personalized ads in
  Settings.
- **Complaint to supervisory authority** — [garanteprivacy.it](https://www.garanteprivacy.it)
  for Italy, or your local authority.

To exercise these rights email
[giampietro.leonoro@gmail.com](mailto:giampietro.leonoro@gmail.com). We
reply within 30 days.

## 6. Minors

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect data from minors. If you are a parent and believe your child
provided data without authorization, contact us for removal.

## 7. Data retention

- Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it from
  the app or uninstall the application.
- AdMob advertising identifiers are managed directly by Google under
  their policy.
- RevenueCat purchase receipts are retained as needed to validate the
  subscription and comply with Apple/Google tax obligations.

---

## 8. Security

Local data is protected by the iOS/Android sandboxing system. Sensitive
data (if any) is stored via `expo-secure-store`, which uses Keychain on
iOS and EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android.
